# Privacy Policy — Nebula Drop

**Last Updated: April 4, 2026**

## Overview

Nebula Drop ("the App") is a casual merge puzzle game developed by Kağan Terzi. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

## Data We Collect

### Automatically Collected
- **Game Progress**: High scores and planet collection data stored locally on your device
- **Device Information**: Basic device type for ad serving (via Google AdMob)

### We Do NOT Collect
- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Photos or camera data
- Contact information
- Health or fitness data

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
- Device advertising identifier (IDFA)
- IP address (for ad targeting)
- General location (country/region level)

For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Apple StoreKit
In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

## Data Storage

All game data (scores, settings, collection progress) is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit game data to external servers.

## Children's Privacy

Nebula Drop is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app displays ads through AdMob which complies with COPPA requirements.

## Your Rights

- **Delete Data**: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data
- **Opt-out of Ads**: Purchase "Remove Ads" in-app to disable all advertisements
- **Ad Tracking**: You can limit ad tracking in iOS Settings → Privacy → Tracking

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
